# 🛡️ Phishing Awareness Training Website

## 📌 Project Overview
The **Phishing Awareness Training Website** is a beginner-friendly cybersecurity project designed to educate users about phishing attacks and how to stay safe online.

This website provides structured learning, real-world examples, and an interactive quiz with certification to enhance user awareness.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Educate users about phishing attacks
- Help identify malicious emails and fake websites
- Explain social engineering tactics used by attackers
- Promote safe online practices
- Provide interactive learning through quizzes

---

## 🌐 Features

### 🏠 1. Home / Introduction
- Overview of phishing
- Importance of cybersecurity awareness
- Clean and modern UI design

---

### 📖 2. What is Phishing?
- Detailed explanation of phishing attacks
- Step-by-step working of phishing
- Real-life examples

---

### 🎣 3. Types of Phishing
- Email Phishing
- Spear Phishing
- Smishing (SMS phishing)
- Vishing (Voice phishing)
- Whaling (targeting executives)

---

### 📧 4. Recognizing Phishing Emails
- Suspicious sender addresses
- Urgent or threatening messages
- Fake links and attachments
- Grammar and spelling errors

---

### 🌍 5. Identifying Fake Websites
- URL manipulation tricks
- HTTP vs HTTPS
- Fake login pages
- Poor website design

---

### 🧠 6. Social Engineering Tactics
- Urgency
- Fear
- Authority
- Trust
- Greed

---

### 🔐 7. Prevention Tips
- Strong password practices
- Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
- Avoid clicking unknown links
- Verify sources
- Keep systems updated

---

### ⚠️ 8. Real-World Examples
- Bank phishing scams
- Fake OTP messages
- Lottery scams
- Job frauds

---
